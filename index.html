Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about NEBULA streaming

Frequently Asked Questions — 6 questions available
1 How do I create a NEBULA account? Account
Creating a NEBULA account is simple. Visit our website, click "Sign Up," and provide your email address and password. You can also sign up using your existing Google or Apple account for faster registration. Once verified, you can start your free trial immediately.
2 What content is available on NEBULA? Content
NEBULA offers thousands of movies and TV shows across all genres including action, drama, comedy, horror, documentaries, and more. Our library includes the latest releases, classic favorites, and exclusive original content. New titles are added regularly to keep our catalog fresh and exciting.
3 What devices can I use to watch NEBULA? Technical
NEBULA is available on all major devices including smartphones (iOS and Android), tablets, smart TVs, laptops, and desktop computers. You can watch on your TV with Apple TV, Roku, Amazon Fire Stick, and Chromecast. Download the NEBULA app from your device's app store or visit nebula.rizoel.com to stream directly from your browser.
4 What internet speed do I need for streaming? Technical
For smooth streaming, we recommend at least 5-8 Mbps for HD quality and 25+ Mbps for 4K Ultra HD. Our adaptive streaming technology automatically adjusts quality based on your connection speed. You can also manually select your preferred quality in the player settings.
5 Can I download content to watch offline? Account
Yes! Premium members can download movies and TV shows to watch offline. Simply tap the download icon next to the title. Downloads are available for 30 days and can be watched on any of your registered devices. This is perfect for travel or when you're away from internet connectivity.
6 Are there subtitles available in multiple languages? Content
Absolutely! Most of our content includes subtitles in multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Japanese, and many more. You can select your preferred subtitle language in the player settings. Audio tracks in different languages are also available for select titles.
